Rise Revolution

Rise Revolution: Empowering Self-Improvement Rise Revolution is a front-end website promoting self-improvement. Developed for the Rise Revolution YouTube challenge, it encourages personal growth through insightful content and resources. Features: Home: Welcomes visitors and invites them to join the movement. About: Provides insight into the founder's journey and commitment to growth. Services: Offers physical, biblical, and mental development services. Contact: Enables direct communication with the Rise Revolution team. Tech Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript Boxicons for icons Google Fonts for typography.
